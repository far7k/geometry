About the Photographer

七里水巷 is a fine art photographer whose work moves between the ephemeral and the eternal — capturing moments where light, architecture, and human presence converge into stillness.

Based between Venice and Shanghai, her practice is rooted in the European photographic tradition: a patient, contemplative approach to seeing. She photographs at dawn and dusk, in the narrow calle of Venice, the courtyards of Vienna, and the mist-covered bridges of old Europe.

Her work has been exhibited in galleries across Italy, France, and China, and featured in international photography journals. She believes a photograph is not taken — it is received.

03
Venice — A Love Letter

The city that floats,
between memory and water.

Venice is not merely a place — it is a condition of light. For five years, I have returned to its islands in every season, chasing the hour when the Grand Canal turns to gold and the calle fall silent.

"In Venice, you learn that beauty is not something you find. It is something that finds you — if you are still enough."

This ongoing series documents the city beyond its postcards: the fishermen at first light, the crumbling facades reflected in green water, the solitude of a piazza emptied by winter fog. Each frame is a conversation with a city that is slowly, gracefully, sinking.
